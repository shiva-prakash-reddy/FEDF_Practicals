About Lord Ganesh

Lord Ganesh

Lord Ganesha, the son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, is worshipped as the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom and prosperity. He is easily recognized by his elephant head, symbolizing knowledge, strength, and focus. People worship Him especially during Ganesh Chaturthi and before starting any new work or journey.

About Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi is a Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha, the god of wisdom and prosperity. People worship Ganesha with prayers, decorations, and sweets like modak. The festival ends with the immersion of the idol in water, symbolizing farewell and new beginnings.
